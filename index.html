Research Areas

Machine Learning Theory

We study the mathematical foundations of deep learning, including generalization, optimization, and robustness — building principled frameworks for reliable AI.

👁️

Computer Vision

Developing next-generation visual perception systems spanning object detection, 3D reconstruction, and scene understanding for real-world deployment.

💬

Natural Language Processing

Building large-scale language and multimodal understanding systems that push the boundaries of human-machine communication and reasoning.
